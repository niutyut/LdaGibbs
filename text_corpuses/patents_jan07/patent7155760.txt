title: Waste trap

abstract: 
A waste trap  for use in plumbing installations is provided, the trap comprising a unitary metal body  defining an interior volume, and having a tubular plastics insert  located in an inlet  of the body  and extending into the volume to below the height of a waste outlet . The insert  partitions the upper portion of the volume into two regions, and serves to provide a water seal between the inlet  and the outlet  when the trap is in use. The insert is non-removably secured to the body.
