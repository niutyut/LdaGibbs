title: Device for opening or closing an opening, in particular a bottom opening of a material bunker for a blast furnace

abstract: 
A device for closing or opening an opening, in particular a filling or bottom opening () of a material bunker () arranged above the furnace throat of a blast furnace, including a tilting disk () connectable to a drive. The tilting disk () is articulated to two swivel arms () pivotably mounted about two swivel axes () extending parallel to each other, and to be more precise, in such a manner that the edge areas of the tilting disk () situated on a perpendicular to the swivel axes () of the two swivel arms (), upon a movement of these arms, move from the closed into the opened position, and vice versa, along oppositely directed arc paths ().
