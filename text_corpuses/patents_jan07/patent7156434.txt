title: Cooking tool assembly

abstract: 
A cooking tool assembly () is disclosed which includes a first cooking tool comprising tong means () having gripping means () located at one end of respective arms () the arms being distally pivotted at the other end, and second cooking tool (spatula () in this embodiment ) associated with the tong means () proximate the other ends of the arms () whereby the assembly constitutes a triple-ended reversible bi-functional cooking tool.
