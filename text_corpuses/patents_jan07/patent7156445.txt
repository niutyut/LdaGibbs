title: Sealing mechanism for convertible top of vehicle

abstract: 
Disclosed is a sealing mechanism for a convertible top of a vehicle, which comprises a sealing cloth  disposed inside a lateral edge of a side zone  of a top fabric  in such a manner as to be gradually inclined toward the laterally inward side of the convertible top, as going from a front side to a rear side in a deployment direction of the convertible top . The sealing cloth  is designed to be brought into contact with a weather strip  in a deployed position. The sealing mechanism of the present invention can ensure high-level sealing between the top fabric and a top frame in a lateral edge of the convertible top.
