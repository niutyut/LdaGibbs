title: Clip for purging and refilling inkjet cartridges

abstract: 
Disclosed is a clip for holding an inkjet cartridge for the duel purposes of either refilling it with ink, or suctioning out the ports on the cartridge's printhead. The clip has an overhang with a depending portion which helps hold the cartridge in place. The clip also has a horizontally-extending portion having a wavy cross-section. The wavy cross-sectional configuration gives the clip its desired flexibility while maintaining good retention of the cartridge.
