title: Golf ball with thermoplastic material

abstract: 
Disclosed herein is novel thermoplastic material and a golf ball utilizing the thermoplastic material. The golf ball () preferably comprises a core (), a cover () and optionally a boundary layer (). At least one of the core (), cover () or boundary layer () of the golf ball () comprises the thermoplastic material. The thermoplastic material comprises a partially to highly neutralized blend of copolymers additionally comprising a fatty acid or fatty acid salt. The golf ball component of the invention has soft feel and resilience that is maintained or improved.
