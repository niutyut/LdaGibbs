title: Method for removing Cryptosporidium oocysts from water

abstract: 
A method for stabilizing and removing oocysts or cysts from water. The method comprises adding chitosan, a salt or solution of chitosan to water containing oocysts or cysts and a halogenating agent. The method may also include adding a secondary polyelectrolyte flocculant to the water. The resulting flocs are filtered to remove the oocysts or cysts.
