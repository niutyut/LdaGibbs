title: Platinum coating process

abstract: 
A process for depositing pure platinum on a substrate is disclosed. In accordance with one embodiment, the process comprises applying a solution consisting of Pt(acetylacetonate)and ethanol or acetone onto a substrate and wrapping at least a portion of the substrate with aluminum foil. The process further comprises heating the substrate wrapped with the aluminum foil to about 300? C. at a rate of about 10?25? C. per minute and then holding at about 300? C. for about 1 hour, wherein the Pt(acetylacetonate)decomposes to deposit pure platinum on the substrate.
