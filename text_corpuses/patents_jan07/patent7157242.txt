title: Method for staining fungi and protozoa

abstract: 
The present invention provides a method for the staining of fungi and for observation with a light microscope based upon the presence of chitin in the composition of these organisms. With the method of the present invention a sample to be analyzed is treated with a solution of Ponceau S and Stains-all dye. The sample is then selectively decolorized and rinsed.
