title: Soybean cultivar SG1431RR

abstract: 
A novel soybean cultivar, designated SG1431RR, is disclosed. The invention relates to the seeds of soybean cultivar SG1431RR, to the plants of soybean SG1431RR and to methods for producing a soybean plant produced by crossing the cultivar SG1431RR with itself or another soybean variety. The invention further relates to hybrid soybean seeds and plants produced by crossing the cultivar SG1431RR with another soybean cultivar.
